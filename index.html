About

I am Daniel Tarsoly, a dedicated programmer with a Bachelor's Degree in Software Development. Currently I am living in Veszprém, Hungary.

My main interests are application, mobile and web development. I put a big emphasis on system planning and like to get involved in the entire stack. I am working hard to continuously broaden my perspectives and learn new technologies.
